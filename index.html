Welcome to Release Control

Think of this like walking into a tight, professional label manager’s office — everything for your music business laid out on the desk: clear, calm, and under control.

What this app is for

  • Indie artists who want to work like a real label.
  • Start-up record labels building their first catalog.
  • Managers and consultants keeping multiple releases on track.

What it helps you do

  • See all releases and business tasks in one place.
  • Follow step-by-step checklists so nothing is missed.
  • Track what’s done, what’s pending, and who needs to move next.

How to use it

  • Start in Releases for each song, EP, or album.
  • Use Business & Publishing to build your company + PRO + admin.
  • Use Contracts and Royalties & Admin as your label-control checklist.
